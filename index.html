St Lasalle School Karemeno is a catholic school under the ownership and management of the De La Salle Christian Brothers (A Religious Congregation in the Catholic Church). It started in the year 2016 and officially opened and blessed by Archbishop Peter Kairo in 2nd July in the same year. St. Lasalle School Karemeno is a sister school to St Mary’s Boys Secondary Nyeri, Rongai Boys Nakuru and St Paul’s Secondary Marsabit. It is a boys and girls boarding school and is open to all irrespective of religious denomination. St Lasalle teaches the regular subjects for the 8-4-4 KCSE National exams.
